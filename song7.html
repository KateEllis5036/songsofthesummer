Song Title

Please Please Please

Performer

Sabrina Carpender

My Review

My favorite song this summer! I absolutely loved it and have been holding all 5 starts in reserve for this song. No. 1 in my heart, Please Please Please delivers incredibly relatable lines, such as my all-time favorite line “Heartbreak is one thing, my ego’s another. I beg you, don’t embarrass me...”, and another classic, relatable line: “If you wanna go and be stupid don’t do it in front of me”. Truly a phenomenal song. A perfect pop-phenomenon in song form with a flawless music video.
