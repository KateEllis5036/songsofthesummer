Song Title

Too Sweet

Performer

Hozier

My Review

A beautiful song! Too Sweet has been one of my top songs since its release. I love a good alternative song, and this one hit all the important marks, with strong lyrics and a wonderful instrumental track. I am particularly fond of the bells in the background, a symbol synonymous with weddings in a song that ultimately sums down to the rejection of a relationship.
